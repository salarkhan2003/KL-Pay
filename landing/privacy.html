🔒 Privacy

Privacy
Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here is exactly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective immediately

1. Introduction

KL ONE ("we", "our", "the Platform") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data. By using KL ONE, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Types of User Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: Full name, student/employee ID, gender
  • Contact data: Email address, mobile phone number
  • Academic data: Hostel name, block, department (KL University affiliation)
  • Transaction data: Order history, payment amounts, Cashfree order IDs, K-Coins balance, payment status
  • Usage data: App interactions, features used, session timestamps, pages visited
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, IP address (for web app security)

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the platform. We do not collect sensitive personal data such as Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, or biometric data.

3. How We Collect Data

  • Directly from you when you register, update your profile, or place an order
  • Automatically when you use the platform (usage and device data via browser APIs)
  • From Cashfree Payments when a payment is processed (transaction confirmation data only — we never receive your UPI PIN, card number, or bank credentials)
  • From Supabase Authentication when you sign in with email and password

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve KL ONE:

  • Process and fulfill food orders
  • Process payments and issue refunds
  • Award and track K-Coins loyalty rewards
  • Send order status notifications (ready for pickup, etc.)
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Improve platform performance, features, and user experience
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating KL ONE.

5. Payment Data & Security

Payment processing is handled entirely by Cashfree Payments, an RBI-regulated payment aggregator. KL ONE does not store, process, or transmit your:

  • UPI PIN or UPI credentials
  • Debit/credit card numbers or CVV
  • Net banking credentials
  • Bank account numbers or IFSC codes

We only store the following payment-related data for record-keeping:

  • Cashfree order IDs (reference numbers)
  • Transaction amounts
  • Payment status (paid / failed / pending)

For Cashfree's data practices, refer to their Privacy Policy.

6. How User Data Is Protected & Stored

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) — an industry-leading cloud database platform with enterprise-grade security:

  • Encryption at rest: All data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Row-level security (RLS): Database policies ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth handles identity management with industry-standard security
  • Access control: Only authorized KL ONE administrators can access user data, and only for support purposes

7. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Cashfree Payments: Your name, phone number, email, and payment amount are shared to process transactions
  • Campus Merchants: Your name and phone number are shared with the merchant when you place a food order, so they can prepare and hand over your order
  • KL University Administration: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics only (no personally identifiable information)
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or government regulation

All third-party service providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the specified purpose.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

KL ONE uses browser local storage to maintain your login session and user preferences. We do not use:

  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Advertising or marketing cookies
  • Cross-site tracking technologies

Session data stored in local storage is cleared when you log out. You can also clear it manually by clearing your browser's site data for our domain.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian financial regulations (Income Tax Act, GST regulations). Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate data via your Profile settings in the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability: Request your transaction data in a machine-readable format (CSV/JSON)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request via the in-app support system or email support@klone.in. We will respond within 30 days.

11. Children's Privacy

KL ONE is intended for university students aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately at support@klone.in.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app and by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact & Grievance Officer

For privacy-related queries, data requests, or complaints:

  • Email: support@klone.in
  • In-app: Profile → Support → New Ticket
  • Address: KL University, Vaddeswaram, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh — 522302, India
  • Response time: Within 30 days of receiving your request
