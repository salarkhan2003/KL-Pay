General
KL ONE is the official campus super-app for KL University. It lets students order food from campus canteens, pay merchants via UPI, earn K-Coins rewards, and track all transactions — all in one place.
KL ONE is available to all enrolled students, faculty, staff, and registered merchants of KL University. You need a KL University email address to sign up.
Yes, KL ONE is free to download and use. A small platform fee of ?2.50 applies per transaction to cover payment processing costs. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.
Click "Get Started" on this page or open the KL ONE app. Sign in with your KL University Google account. Your profile is created automatically on first login.
Ordering
Open KL ONE ? Browse campus outlets ? Select an outlet ? Add items to cart ? Checkout ? Pay via UPI. Your order is confirmed instantly after payment.
Your order status updates in real time in the app. When the merchant marks your order as "Ready", you'll see it immediately in the Orders section. Go to the outlet and show your order token for pickup.
Once payment is processed, orders cannot be cancelled by the student. Only the merchant can cancel an order. If a merchant cancels your order, a refund will be initiated automatically.
Each order gets a unique token number (e.g., #42). Show this token at the canteen counter when picking up your food. It helps merchants identify your order quickly without confusion.
Payments
KL ONE supports all UPI payment methods — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and any bank's UPI app. Payments are processed through Cashfree's secure gateway.
The ?2.50 flat fee covers payment gateway costs and platform maintenance. It's the same regardless of order size — no percentage-based fees.

K-Coins & Rewards
K-Coins are KL ONE's loyalty reward points. You earn 5 K-Coins for every successful food order. They have no monetary value but can be redeemed for campus perks and discounts.
Order food through KL ONE every day to build a streak. Consecutive daily orders earn bonus K-Coins multipliers. The longer your streak, the more you earn per order.
No. K-Coins are non-transferable and tied to your individual account. They cannot be sold, gifted, or redeemed for cash.
Refunds
Go to Support in the app ? Create a new ticket ? Select "Refund Request" ? Include your order ID and reason. Requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the transaction.
Once approved, Cashfree processes the refund within 1–3 business days. It appears in your bank account within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.
