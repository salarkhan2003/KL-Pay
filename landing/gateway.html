Security & Compliance

???

PCI-DSS Level 1

The highest level of payment card industry compliance. Your payment data is handled to the strictest global standards.

???

RBI Regulated

Cashfree is a licensed payment aggregator regulated by the Reserve Bank of India under the Payment and Settlement Systems Act.

??

End-to-End Encryption

All payment data is encrypted using TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest. Your credentials never touch our servers.

?

Webhook Verification

Every payment confirmation webhook from Cashfree is verified using HMAC-SHA256 to prevent tampering or spoofing.

??

Server-side Keys Only

Cashfree API credentials are stored exclusively as server-side environment variables. They are never exposed to the browser or client.

??

Zero Credential Storage

KL ONE never stores UPI PINs, card numbers, CVVs, or any sensitive payment credentials. Ever.
