💸 Refunds

Refund
Policy

Clear, fair, and transparent refund guidelines for all KL ONE transactions.

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

KL ONE processes payments through Cashfree Payments. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both students and merchants while maintaining the integrity of the platform.

Eligible Refund Scenarios

A refund will be issued in the following cases:

  • Merchant cancellation: The merchant cancels your order before preparation begins
  • Item unavailability: A paid item is unavailable and no substitute is offered
  • Duplicate payment: You were charged twice for the same order due to a technical error
  • Payment deducted, order not created: Money was deducted from your account but no order was created in the system
  • System failure: A verified technical failure on our end caused an incorrect charge

Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will NOT be issued for:

  • Change of mind after payment is processed
  • Food quality complaints (these should be raised with the merchant directly)
  • Orders that have already been prepared or marked "Ready"
  • Direct Pay transactions (peer-to-merchant payments)
  • The ₹2.50 platform fee (non-refundable in all cases)
  • K-Coins awarded on a transaction
  • Delays in food preparation

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Open KL ONE and go to Support
  • Create a new support ticket with subject "Refund Request"
  • Include your order ID, payment amount, and reason for refund
  • Requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the transaction

Refund Processing Time

Once a refund is approved:

  • Cashfree processes the refund within 1–3 business days
  • The refund appears in your bank account within 5–7 business days depending on your bank
  • UPI refunds typically reflect faster than card refunds

Refund Amount

Approved refunds are for the food/item amount only. The ₹2.50 platform fee is non-refundable. If K-Coins were awarded on the transaction, they will be deducted from your wallet upon refund approval.

Dispute Resolution

If your refund request is denied and you believe this is incorrect, you may escalate by contacting us through our Contact page. We will review your case within 3 business days.

Contact

For refund queries, use the in-app support system or visit our Contact page.
