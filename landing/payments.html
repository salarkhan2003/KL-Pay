💳 Secure Payments

How Payments Work

Every rupee on KL ONE moves through Cashfree's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure — fast, transparent, and secure.

Payment Gateway

KL ONE is powered by Cashfree Payments — one of India's leading payment gateways trusted by thousands of businesses.

🔒

PCI-DSS Compliant

Cashfree is fully PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. Your payment data is encrypted end-to-end and never stored on our servers.

⚡

Instant UPI Payments

Pay using any UPI app — GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or your bank's UPI. Payments settle in seconds.

🔀

Automatic Split Payments

When you pay, Cashfree automatically splits the amount — vendor gets their share, platform fee goes to admin. Zero manual work.

📡

Real-time Webhooks

Payment confirmation is instant via Cashfree webhooks. Your order status updates the moment payment clears.

🧾

Full Transaction History

Every transaction is logged with a unique Cashfree order ID. Students and merchants can view complete payment history anytime.

🏦

Merchant Settlements

Merchants receive their payment directly to their registered UPI VPA. No delays, no manual transfers.

Payment Flow

From cart to confirmation — here's exactly what happens when you pay.

01

Student places order

Items are added to cart and checkout is initiated from the KL ONE app.

02

Cashfree session created

Our server calls Cashfree API to create a payment session with vendor split configured.

03

Student completes UPI payment

Cashfree's secure checkout handles the UPI transaction. We never see your UPI PIN or credentials.

04

Webhook confirms payment

Cashfree sends a signed webhook to our server. Order status is updated to "paid" in real time.

05

Merchant notified & K-Coins awarded

Merchant sees the order on their dashboard. Student receives 5 K-Coins automatically.

Security Standards

🛡️

256-bit SSL Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3 with 256-bit AES encryption.

🔑

Server-side Credentials

Cashfree API keys are stored exclusively as server-side environment variables. Never exposed to the client.

✅

Webhook Signature Verification

Every Cashfree webhook is verified using HMAC-SHA256 signature to prevent spoofing.

🚫

No Card Data Stored

We never store card numbers, CVVs, UPI PINs, or any sensitive payment credentials.
