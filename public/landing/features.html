✨ Platform Features

Everything in
One App

KL ONE is a complete campus super-app. Here's everything it can do for students, merchants, and admins.

🎓 For Students

Student Features

🏪

Browse Campus Outlets

Discover all campus canteens with live open/closed status, ratings, block location, and food categories.

🛒

Food Ordering

Browse menus, add items to cart, and checkout with UPI in under 30 seconds.

📱

Real-time Order Tracking

Watch your order status update live from pending to ready — no refreshing needed.

🔗

Direct Pay

Pay any campus merchant directly via UPI without placing a food order. Great for quick purchases.

🪙

K-Coins Rewards

Earn 5 K-Coins on every order. Build daily streaks for bonus multipliers.

📊

Transaction History

Full history of all payments, orders, and K-Coins earned — searchable and filterable.

🎫

Order Tokens

Each order gets a unique token. Show it at the counter for fast, confusion-free pickup.

🆘

Support Tickets

Raise a support ticket for any issue — payment problems, wrong orders, or general queries.

🏪 For Merchants

Merchant Features

📊

Live Order Dashboard

See all incoming orders in real time. Accept, prepare, and complete orders from one screen.

🍽️

Menu Management

Add, edit, delete menu items. Toggle availability instantly — changes reflect immediately for students.

🔔

Real-time Alerts

Instant notifications for new orders and direct payments via Socket.io and Firestore.

💰

Automatic UPI Payouts

Payments go directly to your UPI VPA via Cashfree split. No manual collection needed.

⚙️ For Admins

Admin Features

🏗️

Outlet Management

Create, edit, and delete campus outlets. Assign merchants, set categories, and manage timings.

📈

Platform Analytics

View total orders, revenue, platform fees collected, and active users across the campus.

🎫

Support Management

View and resolve all student support tickets. Mark tickets as resolved or closed.

💳

Transaction Oversight

Full visibility into all platform transactions, payment statuses, and K-Coins awarded.
