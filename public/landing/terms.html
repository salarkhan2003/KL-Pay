2. Service Description

KL ONE is a campus super-application that provides the following services to KL University students and merchants:

  • Food Ordering: Browse campus canteen menus, place food orders, and track order status in real time
  • UPI Payments: Make secure digital payments to campus merchants via Cashfree Payments gateway
  • Direct Pay: Send UPI payments directly to any campus merchant
  • K-Coins Rewards: Earn loyalty points on every transaction
  • Merchant Dashboard: Tools for campus merchants to manage menus, orders, and settlements

KL ONE acts solely as a technology intermediary connecting students and campus merchants. We do not prepare, sell, or deliver food items directly.
