💸 Refunds

Refund & Cancellation
Policy

Clear, fair, and transparent refund and cancellation guidelines for all KL ONE transactions.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

KL ONE processes all payments through Cashfree Payments, an RBI-regulated payment aggregator. This Refund and Cancellation Policy applies to all transactions made on the KL ONE platform, including food orders and Direct Pay transactions. Please read this policy carefully before making any payment.

2. Cancellation Policy

By Students: Once a food order is placed and payment is confirmed, it cannot be cancelled by the student. This is because the merchant begins preparation immediately upon payment confirmation.

By Merchants: A merchant may cancel an order if an item is unavailable or if they are unable to fulfill the order. In such cases, a full refund of the food amount will be issued to the student.

Direct Pay Transactions: Direct Pay (peer-to-merchant UPI payments) are non-cancellable once initiated and confirmed.

3. Refund Eligibility

A refund will be issued in the following cases:

  • Merchant cancellation: The merchant cancels your order before or during preparation
  • Item unavailability: A paid item is unavailable and no substitute is offered by the merchant
  • Duplicate payment: You were charged twice for the same order due to a verified technical error
  • Payment deducted, order not created: Money was deducted from your account but no order was created in the system due to a technical failure
  • System failure: A verified technical failure on our end caused an incorrect or excess charge

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will NOT be issued for:

  • Change of mind after payment is processed
  • Food quality complaints (these should be raised directly with the merchant)
  • Orders that have already been prepared or marked "Ready for Pickup"
  • Uncollected orders (orders not picked up within 30 minutes of "Ready" notification)
  • Direct Pay transactions (peer-to-merchant UPI payments) — these are final and non-refundable
  • The ₹2.50 platform convenience fee — non-refundable in all cases
  • K-Coins awarded on a transaction
  • Delays in food preparation time
  • Payment failures caused by your bank, UPI app, or network issues

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Open KL ONE and navigate to Profile → Support → New Ticket
  • Select subject: "Refund Request"
  • Provide your Order ID, transaction amount, date, and reason for refund
  • Requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the transaction
  • Alternatively, email support@klone.in with the subject "Refund Request — [Order ID]"

6. Refund Processing Timeframes

Once a refund is approved by our team:

  • Cashfree initiates the refund within 1–3 business days of approval
  • The refund reflects in your bank account within 5–7 business days depending on your bank
  • UPI refunds typically reflect faster (1–3 business days) than card refunds
  • You will receive a confirmation notification once the refund is processed

7. Refund Amount

Approved refunds cover the food/item amount only. The ₹2.50 platform convenience fee is non-refundable in all cases. If K-Coins were awarded on the transaction being refunded, they will be deducted from your K-Coins wallet upon refund approval.

8. Dispute Resolution

If your refund request is denied and you believe this decision is incorrect:

  • Reply to your support ticket requesting escalation
  • Email support@klone.in with subject "Refund Dispute — [Ticket ID]"
  • We will review your case within 3 business days and provide a final decision

9. Contact

For refund queries:

  • Email: support@klone.in
  • In-app: Profile → Support → New Ticket
  • Address: KL University, Vaddeswaram, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh — 522302, India
  • Support hours: Monday – Saturday, 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM IST
